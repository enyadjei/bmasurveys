About BMA Survey Consult

BMA Survey Consult was founded to redefine real estate practice by combining technology, accuracy, and professional integrity. We deliver tailored property valuation, land surveying, and real estate consultancy services to clients locally and internationally. Our mission is to enhance the property industry through innovative solutions and reliable data-driven insights — helping clients make smarter real estate decisions.

Headquarted in Tse Addo, Labadi in Accra, the firm has professionals with a collective resource of 50 years of experience in the industy. BMA Surveys has relevant experience in Public and Private Real Estate and Lands Sector of Ghana and the sub-region. BMA places itself as a resource, this attribute allows BMA to effectively engage other stakeholders in ensures the most efficient process-oriented service delivery. BMA seeks to be the most resourceful and efficient private real estate resource in Ghana by 2030.
